Clock

Timekeeper of Your Secrets

Clock Vault is not just a clock app, it's a secure sanctuary for your digital life. It's a unique blend of a regular clock app with a secure vault that allows you to keep your files, documents, photos, videos, and music safe.

Key Features

  • Beautiful clock interface with both analog and digital displays
  • Innovative security through clock hand password system
  • Secure vault for private file storage
  • Support for various file types (photos, videos, documents, music)
  • Simple drag-and-drop file management
  • Complete privacy with local-only storage

Crimson Chord

Description

Crimson Chord is a user-friendly music player designed to load and play all MP3 audio songs stored on your device's local storage. The app features an intuitive interface, making it easy to navigate and enjoy your music collection. It supports background play, allowing you to listen to your favorite tracks while using other applications.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
  • Plays all MP3 audio songs from local storage
  • Supports background play
  • Intuitive music controls
  • Smooth playback experience

Why Choose Crimson Chord?

Crimson Chord offers a seamless music playback experience with its clean interface and efficient performance.
