CLOCK App Tutorial

CLOCK is a secret vault-like app designed to keep your files safe. Here's how to use it:

  1. Open the CLOCK app on your device.
  2. To set your password, simply drag the hour and minute hands to your desired positions.
  3. Remember the time you set, as this will be your password.
  4. To access your hidden files later, open the CLOCK app again.
  5. Move the clock hands to match your password time.
  6. Once the correct time is set, your secret files will be accessible.

This innovative method provides a unique and visually intuitive way to secure your private files. Always remember your set time to ensure you can access your protected content.
